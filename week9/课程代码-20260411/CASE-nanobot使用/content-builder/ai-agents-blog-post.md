# What Are AI Agents? (And Why They're More Than Just Chatbots)

Forget everything you thought you knew about AI assistants. Today's AI agents aren't just conversation partners—they're autonomous problem-solvers that can plan, reason, and act across multiple tools.

## Beyond Simple Queries

Traditional chatbots respond to prompts. AI agents, however, break down complex goals into steps: research market data, analyze financial reports, draft an email, and even schedule follow-ups—all without constant human direction. Think of them as digital colleagues who understand context and execute multi-step workflows.

## Real-World Impact

Companies use AI agents for customer support escalation routing, automated code testing, and personalized learning paths. Unlike static scripts, agents adapt to new information and learn from outcomes—making them increasingly valuable as business complexity grows.

The key difference? Agents *do*, while chatbots *respond*.

As AI capabilities mature, the question isn't whether agents will transform workflows—it's which processes you'll empower them to own first.
